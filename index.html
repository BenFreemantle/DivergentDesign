About Me

Hi my name is Ben i am 20 years old, i currently live in a small town called Newbury south-east of England.

I'm a self-taught Developer in various programming languages and continuously learning everyday, some varying in level of understanding but 'Rome wasn't built in a day' right ? JAVA is my language of choice but I am looking to start learning C# or a functional lanuage like PYTHON or HASKELL soon.

About The Site

I have created this site for multiple reasons.
One: I have never actually made a full website before, they've all been quick prototyping or one of my fickle project ideas that never made it.
Two: to expand my knowlege of HTML and CSS, I have always liked the idea of being able to write beautfiul websites that run smooth with a clear flow, but you can only get there by try, try and trying again with each new iteration knowing more and more.
Three: Somewhere to share my stuff. It's all good having stuff to share but without anywhere to share it to is a little silly. Ofcourse I could just use pre-existing media sites like 'Facebook' or 'Twitter' but there is no personality on sites like that, you have no control on how you want your content to be displayed.
